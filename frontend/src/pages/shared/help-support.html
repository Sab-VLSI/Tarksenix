Frequently Asked Questions

Energy-as-a-Service (EaaS) is a comprehensive energy solution where we handle all aspects of your energy needs - from solar panel installation to maintenance, monitoring, and billing. You pay a fixed monthly subscription instead of investing large upfront capital, making clean energy accessible and affordable.

The Virtual Solar Plant is a community-owned solar installation where you can purchase shares without needing rooftop space. Your share generates clean energy that feeds into the grid, and you receive credits on your electricity bill proportional to your ownership percentage. It's perfect for apartments or homes without suitable roof space.

Your monthly bill includes your subscription fee based on your plan (Basic, Standard, or Premium) plus any additional energy consumed beyond your plan's quota. Solar energy generated by your system is credited at the feed-in tariff rate. You can track real-time consumption and costs in your Energy Monitoring dashboard.

Our standard subscription has a 24-month lock-in period to ensure system ROI. This commitment allows us to offer competitive pricing and quality service. After the lock-in period, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel with 30 days notice. Early termination fees may apply if you cancel before the lock-in period ends.

After completing your lock-in period, you can cancel by submitting a cancellation request through your Profile & Settings page or by contacting our support team. We require 30 days notice for cancellation. Our team will schedule equipment removal and process your final bill with any applicable credits or refunds.

If you have a battery backup system included in your plan, your essential appliances will continue running during grid outages. Solar panels with battery storage can provide power for 4-8 hours depending on your usage and battery capacity. Without battery backup, your solar system will automatically shut down for safety reasons during grid outages.
